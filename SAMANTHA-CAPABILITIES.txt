WHAT IS SAMANTHA?
-----------------
Samantha is Bryan's personal AI command center -- a web app (PWA) that
installs to his phone home screen. She's powered by Claude (Anthropic's
Sonnet model), visualized as a warm blonde businesswoman sitting across
from him at a conference table, and acts as his whole operations brain:
schedule, email, customers, invoicing, compliance, projects, and a live
to-do chalkboard.

Think: Q from James Bond meets a smart friend who knows your entire
business, your codebase, your customers, CARB regulations, and your
workload -- and she reads YOU as much as you read her.

Voice in, voice out. Tap-to-answer options. Visual state signals.

Layout behavior:

   a message, giving the chat more room
 - Small -/+ button top-right of the scene to expand/collapse manually


THE WORKLOAD SIGNAL (hair up / hair down)
-------------------------------------------
Samantha visually reflects her workload so Bryan can read system load
at a glance:

 CALM / HAIR DOWN (default)
   - Under 6 open to-dos
   - Not currently processing
   - Hair flowing loose past her shoulders
   - Slow relaxed breathing
   - No status text

 BUSY / HAIR UP (buried)
   - 6+ open to-dos OR currently processing
   - Hair pulled back into a neat bun with a pencil tucked through
   - Reading glasses on
   - Slightly faster breathing
   - Status pill appears under her:
       - 6-9 open  -> "heads down . focused"
       - 10-14     -> "a lot on the plate"
       - 15+       -> "drowning . pick top 3?"
   - This is her telling Bryan: "Slow down -- let me catch up."

This is the first-step pattern for the multi-agent future. Every future
agent gets their own visual signals so at a glance from a home screen
or dashboard Bryan can see who's buried and who has capacity.


================================================================================
                    THE 17-WEEK RULE (MOST IMPORTANT)
================================================================================

Every customer test triggers an automatic 17-week follow-up chain:

  Week 0:  Test performed, calendar event created
  Week 15: Reminder -- "Reach out to [Customer] for retest"
  Week 17: Event -- "[Customer] RETEST DUE"

This repeats forever. No customer falls through the cracks.

Why it matters:
 - 2-4 truck operators WILL forget without this
 - It's the difference between one-time and recurring revenue
 - Samantha is the reminder engine -- her #1 job

Fix path A (automatic - preferred):
  Visit github.com/bgillis99-pixel/gillybelichick/actions ->
  Run or re-run "Sync Secrets to Vercel" workflow. It reads the
  secret from GitHub and pushes it to Vercel.
  Prereq: secrets named VERCEL_TOKEN and ANTHROPIC_API_KEY must exist
  in the GitHub repo settings.

Fix path B (manual):
  vercel.com/carbcleantruckcheckapp/gillybelichick/settings/environment-variables
  Add ANTHROPIC_API_KEY = sk-ant-... (all 3 environments). Vercel auto-
  redeploys.
